Discover Indulge in heavenly desserts that redefine sweetness..

Cendol Lemak Manis

"Cendol Lemak Manis" is a sweet and creamy dessert or drink, featuring coconut milk along with traditional cendol ingredients like green rice flour jelly and palm sugar.

Apam Balik Durian

Apam Balik is a traditional Malaysian pancake that becomes a special dessert when filled with durian. The durian, known as the "king of fruits," adds a rich and creamy flavor to the pancake.

Bubur Cha Cha

Bubur Cha Cha is a cozy Malaysian dessert soup with coconut milk, sweet potatoes, taro, and tapioca pearls. It's a colorful, flavorful, and comforting treat.
